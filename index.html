BOARD OF CONTROL OF CRICKET IN INDIA(BCCI)

In India the governing body for cricket isBOARD OF CRICKET CONTROL IN INDIA which is also abbreviated as BCCI. It is an autonomous organisation and doesnot comes under NATIONAL SPORTS FEDERATION OF INDIA

BCCI's headquater is situated at the WANKHEDE STADIUM, MUMBAI. BCCI is one of the richest board of the world in cricket. It has three official cricket that represents India. They are INDIAN MEN's NATIONAL CRICKET TEAM, INDIAN WOMEN's NATIONAL CRICKET TEAM, INDIAN NATIONAL UNDER-19 CRICKET TEAM.

BCCI was founded in the year 1928. The current president of BCCI is SOURAV GANGULY.
